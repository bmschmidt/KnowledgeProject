CALLCOTT, Mrs Maria Graham (1786-1844), daughter of Admiral Dundas, became the wife of Sir Augustus Callcott in 1827. With her first husband, Capt. Graham, R.N., she travelled in India, South Africa, and South America, "where she acted for some time as teacher of Donna Maria, who became queen of Portugal in 1826; and in the company of her second husband she spent much time in the south of Europe.
