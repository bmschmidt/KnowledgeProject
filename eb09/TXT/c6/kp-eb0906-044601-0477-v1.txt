COSMAS, of Prague (1045-1126), a Bohemian priest and historian, wrote a Chronicon Bohemorum, which contains the history and traditions of his country up to nearly the time of his death. This work was printed in 1602, and again among the Scriptores Rerum Bohemicarum (Prague, 1783).
