MOUNT VERNON, a city of the United States, county seat of Knox county, Ohio, and a railway junction on the Baltimore and Ohio and the Cleveland, Mount Vernon, and Columbus Railroads, 45 miles north-north-east of [9:17:5] Columbus. Settled in 1805, it had become a well-built flourishing place of 5249 inhabitants in 1880, engaged in various manufacturing industries.
