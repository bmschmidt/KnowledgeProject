EUDOXUS, of Cyzicus, a Greek navigator who flourished about 130 B.c. He was employed by Ptolemy Euergetes to make a voyage to India. After two of these he circumnavigated Africa from the Red Sea to Gades. An attempt to make the return voyage was unsuccessful.
