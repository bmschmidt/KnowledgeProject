LAURIA, or Loria, a city of Italy in the province of Potenza, 13 miles south of Lagonegro, consisting of a walled town on the steep side of a hill and another portion in the [9:14:350] plain below. The castle was the birthplace of Ruggiero di Loria, the great Italian admiral of the 13th century. The population was 10,609 in 1871.
