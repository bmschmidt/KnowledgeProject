BEUTHEN, or Nieder Beuthen, a town in the government of Liegnitz, in Silesia, on the Oder, and the capital of the mediatized principality of Carolath-Beuthen. The chief industries of the place are straw-plaiting, boatbuilding, and the manufacture of pottery; and a considerable traffic is carried on by means of the river. Population in 1871, 3826
