BEL, or Belius, Matthias, an Hungarian divine and historian, was born in 1684, and was educated partly at Halle. In 1719 he was made rector of the evangelical Lyceum at Presburg, where he remained till his death in 1749. His great work was the History of Hungary (Notitia Hungarioe novae historico-geographicd), 4 vols., 1735-42, which was not completed.
