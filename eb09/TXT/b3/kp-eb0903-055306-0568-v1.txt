BELSHAM, William, brother of the preceding, was born in 1752, and died in 1827. His productions were mainly historical and political writings, advocating the politics of the Whig party. Several detached historical treatises were collected together, and published in 1806 under the title, History of Great Britain to the conclusion of the Peace of Amiens in 1802, 12 vols.
