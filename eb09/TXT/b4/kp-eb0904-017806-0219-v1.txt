BOVINO (the ancient Vebinum or Vibonium), a fortified town of Italy, province of Capitanata, 18 miles S.S.W. of Foggia. It is the seat of a bishopric and of a court of primary jurisdiction, and has a cathedral and several churches and convents. Here the Imperialists defeated the Spaniards in 1734. Population, 7088.
