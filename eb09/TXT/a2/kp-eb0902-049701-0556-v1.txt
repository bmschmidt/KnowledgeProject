ARGONNE, a rocky and forest-clad plateau in the north-east of France, extending along the borders of Lorraine and Champagne, and forming part of the departments of Ardennes and Meuse. The famous defence of the French frontier by Dumouriez in 1792 is called the Argonne Campaign.
