ANWEILER, or Annweiler, a town of Bavaria, in the Rhine Palatinate, situated on the Queich, 8 miles W. of Landau, containing 2734 inhabitants, who are chiefly employed in cloth weaving, brushmaking, tanning, and dyeing. Near it is the castle of Trifels, in which Richard Coeur de Lion was imprisoned in 1193.
