CHILMAREE (in Hindustani, ChalamarÎ¹), a town of British India, in the presidency of Bengal, about 35 miles south-east of Rungpur, on the right bank of the Brahmaputra. It is mainly remarkable as the seat of a great religious and commercial festival, which brings together no fewer that from 60,000 to 100,000 people.
