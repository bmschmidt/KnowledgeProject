HERZBERG, a town of the kingdom of Hanover, the capital of a bailiwick of the same name, in the province of Grubenhagen, on the river Sieben. It stands at the foot of the Hartz Mountains, and contains 321 houses, with 2720 inhabitants, employed in cotton and linen weaving, and in making paper.
