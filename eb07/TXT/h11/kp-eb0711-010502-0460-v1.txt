HADERSLEBEN, a city of Denmark, the capital of the bailiwick of the same name, in the province of Sleswick. The harbour has within a few years been nearly choked up, and cargoes are discharged by boats. It contains 400 houses, with about 3600 inhabitants. Long. 9. 25. E. Lat. 55. 15. N.
