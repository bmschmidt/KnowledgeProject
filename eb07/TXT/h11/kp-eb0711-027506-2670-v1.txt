HERISAU, a town, the capital of the canton of Appenzell, in Switzerland. It stands in a beautiful district, is well built, and contains 750 houses and 7000 inhabitants. It is a place of extensive manufactures, especially of cotton goods for the fairs of Germany. Long. 9. 11. 5. E. Lat. 47. 23. 16. N.
