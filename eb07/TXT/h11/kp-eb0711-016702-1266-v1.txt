HATCHEL, or Hitchel, in the manufactory of flax or hemp, a tool, not unlike a card, for dressing and combing them into fine hairs. Hitch els consist of sharp-pointed iron pins, or teeth, set orderly in a board. Of these there are several sorts, some with finer and shorter teeth, others with coarser and longer.
