ILKESTON, a town of the county of Derby, in the hundred of Morleston and Litchurch, 126 miles from London. It is a place of manufacturing industry. The population amounted in 1801 to 2422, in 1811 to 2970, in 1821 to 3681, and in 1831 to 4446.
