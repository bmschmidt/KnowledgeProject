IULUS, a son of Ascanius, born in Lavinium. In the succession to the kingdom of Alba, Aeneas Sylvius, the son of Aeneas and Lavinia, was preferred to him. But he was, by way of compensation, made chief priest.

Iulus, a genus of insects of the order aptera. See Entomology, Index.
