ISCA Dumniorum, in Ancient Geography, a town in Britain; now Exeter, capital of Devonshire. Lat. 5° 44', Long. 3° 40 z W.

Isca Silurum, in Ancient Geography, the station of the Legio Augusta Secunda in Britain; now Caerleon, a town of Monmouthshire, on the Uske.
