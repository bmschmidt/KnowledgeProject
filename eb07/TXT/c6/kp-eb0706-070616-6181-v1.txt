CITTA DI CASTELLO, a city of the papal dominions in Italy, in the delegation of Perugia. It stands on the: Tiber, and contains a cathedral and nine other churches,; twelve monasteries, nine nunneries, and 6000 inhabitants, who are employed in cultivating wine, oil, and silk.
