CHITTAPET, a small town of Hindustan, in the Carnatic, seventy-five miles north-west from Madras, and fifty north-west from Pondicherry. It sustained several sieges during the wars in the Carnatic, and was finally taken by Colonel Coote after the battle of Wandiwash. Long. 79. 26. E. Lat. 12. 25. N.
