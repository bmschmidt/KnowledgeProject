CASTLE-DOUGLAS, a village of Scotland, in the stewartry of Kirkcudbright. It consists of one principal street, lying along the public road, and some back streets, composed of good houses, neatly built. It possesses a post-office, one bank, two branch banks, and a large grain market, held every Monday. This place is in a very thriving condition. It is distant ten miles north-east of Kirkcudbright.
