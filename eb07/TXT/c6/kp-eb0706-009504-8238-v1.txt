CANONRY, the benefice filled by a canon. It differs from a prebend, in that the prebcnd may subsist without the canonicate, whereas the canonicatc is inseparable from the prebend; again, the right of suffrages, and other privileges, are annexed to the canonicate, and not to the prebend.
