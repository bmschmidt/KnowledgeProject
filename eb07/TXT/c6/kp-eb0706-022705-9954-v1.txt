CASTELLANE, an arrondissement of the department of the Lower Alps, in France, 554 square miles in extent. It is divided into six cantons and forty-six communes, containing 2025 inhabitants. The chief place, of the same name, is a city situated on the river Verdon, and containing 1980 inhabitants. Long. 6. 39. E. Lat. 43. 55. N.
