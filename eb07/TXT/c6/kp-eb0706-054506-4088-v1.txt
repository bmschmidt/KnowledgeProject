CHILLICOTHE, a post town, and capital of Ross eounty, Ohio, on the western bank of the Scioto, forty-five miles in a right line from its mouth. It is pleasantly situated on the borders of an elevated and fertile plain, regularly laid out, the streets crossing each other at right angles. This town is a flourishing place, and contains several elegant public buildings. In its vicinity there are a number of valuable mills. The population amounts to nearly 6000. Long. 82. 57. W. Eat. 39. 18. N.
