CARAMNASSA, a small winding river of Hindustan, which separates the province of Batar from that of Benares. It is chiefly remarkable as it is so far different from other streams that the Hindus are forbidden to touch it; and they always cross it therefore with the utmost caution, as by touching it they lose the fruits of their former austerities and pilgrimages.
