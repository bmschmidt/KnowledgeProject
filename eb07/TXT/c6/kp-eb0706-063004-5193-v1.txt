CHRISTIANSUND, a town in Norway, in the bailiwick of Romdals, and province of DrontheiÎ¹n. It stands on an island, or rather three small islands, and a point of land between rocks and cliffs. It has a very secure harbour for a fleet of the largest ships. It contains 400 houses, all of wood, and 1624 inhabitants, employed in shipping and in the fisheries. Long. 17. 37. 15. E. Lat. 63. 6. 55. N.
