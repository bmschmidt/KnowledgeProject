AGUILAR, a town in the arrondissement of Campena, and province of Cordova, in Spain. It is situated on the river Cabra, has 1600 inhabitants, and produces abundance of oil.

Aguilar, a town of Spain, in the province of Navarre, about 24 miles west from Estella.

AGUiLÎ‘R del Campo, a town of Old Castile, with the title of marquisate, about 15 leagues north of the city of Burgos.
