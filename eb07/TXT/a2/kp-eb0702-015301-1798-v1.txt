ADJUTANT, in the military art, is an officer whose business it is to assist the major. Eaeh battalion of foot and regiment of horse has an adjutant, who receives the orders every night from the brigade-major; which, after carrying them to the colonel, he delivers out to the serjeants. λVhen detachments are to be made, he gives the number to be furnished by eaeh company or troop, and assigns the hour and plaee of rendezvous.
