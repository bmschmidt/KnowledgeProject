ALBACETE, a Spanish town in the province of Murcia. It has 1 parish church, 5 monasteries, 1 hospital, and 5200 inhabitants. There are looms for weaving cloth, 28 manufactories of cutlery, a famous fair held in September, and excellent wine here. Long. 2. 2. W. Lat. 38. 51. N.
