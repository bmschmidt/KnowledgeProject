PADIHAM, a township and chapelry in the parish of Whalley, in the hundred of Blackburn, of the county of Lancaster, 213 miles from London. In 1801 the population amounted to 2118, in 1811 to 2556, in 1821 to 3060, and in 1831 to 3529.
