PALLISER, Cape, on the south coast of Eaheinomau-we, is the most northern island of New Zealand, and the north-east point of Cook’s Strait. Long. 183. 58. W. Lat. 4L 34. S.

Palliser’s Islands, a cluster of small islands in the South Pacific Ocean; the largest about fifteen miles long and nine broad, being connected by a reef of coral rocks, difficult of access. Long. 146. 30. W. Lat. 13. 38. S.
