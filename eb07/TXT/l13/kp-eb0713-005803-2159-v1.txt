LANESBOROUGH, a town of Ireland, in the county of Longford, pleasantly situated on the river Shannon, over which there is here a fine bridge. It has a handsome church, and a barrack for a troop of horse. It is situated sixty-two miles north-north-west from Dublin. Long. 8. 5. W. Lat. 52. 36. N.
