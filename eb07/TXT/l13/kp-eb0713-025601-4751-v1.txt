LEUZE, a city of the Netherlands, in the province of Hennegau, and arrondissement of Tournay. It is situated on the river Dender, about six miles from its source, contains 4500 inhabitants, and has a considerable trade in linen. Long. 3. 33. E. Lat. 50. 34. N.
