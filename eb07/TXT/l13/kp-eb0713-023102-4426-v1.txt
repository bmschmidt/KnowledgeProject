LEOBSHL 1 TZ, a city, the capital of a circle of the same name, in Prussian Silesia, situated at the foot of the mountains. It is surrounded with walls, and contains five Catholic churches, an hospital, 530 houses, and 3670 inhabitants employed in making woollens, hosiery, and linen goods.
