LOTOPHAGI, in Ancient Geography, a people of the [7:13:569] Regio Syrtica (so called from their living upon the lotus), ⅛ιhabiting the country between the two Syrtes, from the Cinyphus to the Triton. The lotus was said to be so luscious a food as to make strangers forget their native country.
