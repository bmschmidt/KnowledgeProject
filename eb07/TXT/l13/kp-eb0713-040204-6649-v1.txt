LIXIVIUM, in Pharmacy, a ley obtained by pouring some liquor upon the ashes of plants. It is more or less powerful in proportion as it has imbibed the fixed salts contained in the ashes.

Lizard, in Geography, a cape or promontory of Cornwall, situated, according to the most common computation, in long. 5. 47. W. and lat. 49. 50. N.
