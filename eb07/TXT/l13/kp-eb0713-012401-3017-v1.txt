LAUINGEN, a city of Bavaria, in the circle of the Upper Danube, the capital of a bailiwick of the same name. It is walled, and has 733 houses, with 3530 inhabitants, who make linen and woollen cloths, and have much trade by the Danube, on which it is situated.
