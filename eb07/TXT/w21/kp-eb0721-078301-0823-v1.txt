WARYE, an open town of Hindustan, in the province of Gujerat and district of Werrear, fourteen miles south-west from Ruhdunpoor. It is the residence of many of the head jhuts, and can muster from 800 to 1000 horsemen, well mounted, and armed with spears and sabres.
