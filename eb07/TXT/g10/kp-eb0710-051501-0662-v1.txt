GIBBOUS, a term in Medicine, denoting any protuberance or convexity of the body, such as hunched or humpbacked.

Gibbous, in Astronomy, a term used in reference to the enlightened parts of the moon, while she is moving from the first quarter to the full, and from the full to the last quarter; for all that time the dark part appears horned or falcated, and the illuminated part hunched out, convex, or gibbous.
