CORPUSCLE, in Physics, a minute particle, or physical atom, such as a natural body is composed of. By this word is not meant the elementary particles, nor hypostatical principles of chemists; but those particles, whether of a simple or compound nature, the parts of which will neither be dissolved nor dissipated by ordinary degrees of heat.
