COPY, in a law sense, a transcript of a writing or instrument, made for the use and satisfaction of some of the parties concerned, or in order to preserve the document itself.

Copy is also used for an imitation of any original work,, particularly a painting, draught, figure, or the like.

Copy, among printers, denotes the manuscript or original of a book put into the hands of the compositor.
