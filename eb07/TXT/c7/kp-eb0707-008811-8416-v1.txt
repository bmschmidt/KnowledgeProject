COLNA, a town of Bengal, and district of Jessore, pleasantly situated on the south-west bank of the Muddmutty or Naugunga river, which is navigable at all seasons of the year. It is situated eighty-three miles north-east from Calcutta. Long. 89. 38. E. Lat. 23. 11. N.
