CORBEIL, an arrondissement of the department of the Seine and Oise, in France. Its extent is 420 square miles; and it is divided into four communes, containing 51,750 inhabitants. The city which gives name to the circle is situated at the junction of the Essonne with the Seine, and contains 800 houses, and 3404 inhabitants, who carry on a great trade in corn, and manufacture leather and linen cloth. Long. 4. 21. Ä½. Lat. 48. 48. N.
