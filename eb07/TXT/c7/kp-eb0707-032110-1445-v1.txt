CORBEILS, in Fortification, little baskets about a foot and a half in height, and eight inches wide at the bottom by twelve at the top. These being filled with earth, are frequently set one against another upon the parapet or elsewhere, leaving intervening spaces open, through which to fire upon the enemy under cover.
