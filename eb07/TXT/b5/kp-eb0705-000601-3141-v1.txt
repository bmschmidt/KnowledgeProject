BORISSOGLEBSK, a circle of the Russian government of Tambow, extending over 2412 square miles, with a population of 65,400 persons. The chief place, a city of the same name, on the river Khoper, contains about 500 houses and 3300 souls. Long. 42. 1. E. Lat. 51. 23. N.
