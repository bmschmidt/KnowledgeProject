BRONTE, a city in the intendency of Catania, in Sicily. It stands in a healthy situation on the river Giaretta, near a celebrated water-fall. It is an industrious place, where linen and woollen cloths, and some kinds of paper, are made. Good wine is produced in the neighbourhood. It gave the title of duke to the late Lord Nelson. The population amounts to 9400 persons.
