JELEZ, a town of the province of Orel, in Russia, the capital of a circle of the same name, on the river Alt. It is a very ancient city, containing twelve stone and two wooden churches, 1290 houses, and 7950 inhabitants. Long. 38. 33. E. Lat. 52. 38. N.
