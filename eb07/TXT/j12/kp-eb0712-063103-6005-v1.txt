JUNGER POOR, a town of Hindustan, possessed by native chiefs, situated in the province of Gujerat, ninety miles north-east from Ahmedabad. These chiefs are occasionally tributary to the Mahrattas. Long. 73. 38. E. Lat. 23. 49. N.
