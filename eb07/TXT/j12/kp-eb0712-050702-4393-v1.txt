JAMPOL, a town of the province of Podolia, in Russia, the capital of a circle of the same name, and situated on the river Dnieper. It is the frontier town towards the Turkish dominions, and has a quarantine and customhouse. Long. 28. 24. Ä½. Lat. 48. 15. N.
