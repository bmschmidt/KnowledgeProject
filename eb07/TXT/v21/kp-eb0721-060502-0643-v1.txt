VERSETZ, a city of Hungary, the capital of a circle of the same name in the province of Hither Theiss. It is the seat of a Greek bishop, and a place of great industry, having large silk mills, and an extensive trade in wine, corn, rice, silk, and other commodities. Here are extensive barracks for cavalry. It contains many churches, 2600 houses, and 16,200 inhabitants. Lat. 45. 4.6. Long. 21. 13. 40. E.
