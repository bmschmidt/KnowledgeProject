RAS EL KHYMA a town of Arabia, OnthePersianGulf, situated on a sandy peninsula, the isthmus of which is defended by a battery, while the Sea-line is also fortified by batteries of one gun each at regular intervals. It has a suburb of bamboo huts, roofed with date-tree leaves.
