RIETI one of the delegations or provinces into which the papal dominions are divided. It is a wild and moun[7:19:242]tainous district, containing two cities, sixteen market-towns, with many villages and hamlets, and 65,750 inhabitants. The capital is the city of the same name situated on the river Belino, near its junction with the Turano. It contains ten parish churches, five monasteries, seven nunneries, and 9270 inhabitants, who subsist chiefly by the trade in silk, and by tanning and currying leather. Long. 12. 46. 50. E. Lat. 42. 24. 25. N.
