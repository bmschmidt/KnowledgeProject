RAYLEIGH a town of the county of Essex, in the hundred of Rochford, thirty-five miles from London. It stands on a creek of the Thames called Hadley Bay, in consequence of which it has a good trade in corn. The popu[7:19:79]Iation amounted in 1801 to 897, in 1811 to 1131, in 1821 to 1203, and in 1831 to 1339.
