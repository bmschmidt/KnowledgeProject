REST-HARROW or Cammock, the Ononis Arvensis. A decoction of this plant has been much recommended for horses labouring under a stoppage of urine. It is the pest of some corn-fields; but in its younger state, before the plant has acquired thorns, it is a most acceptable food for sheep.
