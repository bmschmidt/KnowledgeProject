AXINOMANCY, Axinomantia, from αξ∕vη, securis, and μuντuα, divinatio; an ancient species of divination, or a method of foretelling future events by means of an ax or hatchet. This art was in considerable repute amongst the ancients, and was performed, according to some, by laying an agate stone on a red-hot hatchet, and also by fix ing a hatchet on a round stake so as to be exactly poised; then the names of those suspected were repeated, and he at whose name the hatchet moved was pronounced j guilty. χ
