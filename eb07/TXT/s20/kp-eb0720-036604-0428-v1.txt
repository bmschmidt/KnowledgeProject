SINGAN, a city of China of the first rank, in the frontier province of Shan-si. It formed at one time the imperial residence, and is still a very large and fine city. It is the rendezvous of the troops appointed to defend the empire on the north-western side. Long. 108. 29. E. Lat. 35. 14. N.
