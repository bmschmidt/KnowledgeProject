SELIGENSTADT, a city of the grand duchy of Hesse-Darmstadt, in Germany, in the province of Starkenburg, and the capital of a bailiwick of the same name. It stands on the river Maine, is surrounded with walls, and contains an ancient monastery, 420 houses, and 2640 inhabitants.
