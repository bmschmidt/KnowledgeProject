TERVENGARY, a small Mahommedan town of Hindustan, in the province of Malabar, situated on the banks of a river which is navigable in the rainy season thirty-two miles for canoes. Near this town Tippoo’s forces were decisively defeated by Colonel Hartley’s army. It is twentythree miles south by east from Calicut. Long. 76. E. Lat. 11. 2. N.
