TERRACINA, a city of Italy, in the papal delegation of Frosinone. It stands on the sea-shore, at the foot of a hill, where the Pontine marshes terminate. It is the seat of a bishop, has a cathedral, and is surrounded with walls; and being a frontier place, is furnished with a garrison. It is deemed unhealthy, but contains 4170 inhabitants.
