TRINITY, in Theology, the ineffable mystery of three persons in one Godhead; the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Trinity-Sunday, a festival observed on the Sunday next after Whitsunday, in honour of the holy Trinity. The observation of this festival was first enjoined in the council of Arles, in the year 1260.

T rinity Bay, a deep bay on the north-east coast of New Holland, between Cape Grafton and Cape Tribulation.
