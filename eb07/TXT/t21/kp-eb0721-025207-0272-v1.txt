THRAPSTON, a town in the hundred of Navisford and county of Northampton, seventy-five miles from London. It is built on the river Nen, by which it has some trade with Lynn and with Northampton. There is a market on Tuesday. The inhabitants amounted in 1821 to 854, and in 1831 to 1014.
