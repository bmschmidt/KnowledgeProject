MAHRISCH-KROMAU, a town of the circle of Znaym, in the Austrian province of Moravia, situated on the river Rokitna. There is here an ancient castle belonging to the Lichtenstein family, with a curious collection of antique weapons and armour; and in the church is the burial-place of that house. It contains 204 houses, and 1390 inhabitants, whose principal occupation consists in digging coal and making potash.
