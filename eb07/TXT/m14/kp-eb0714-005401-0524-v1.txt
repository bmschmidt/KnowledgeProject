MALATIA, a town of Asia Minor, situated in a fine plain, about fifteen miles west of the Euphrates. It stands between that river and the Melas, on the site of the ancient Mclitene, now in ruins. The modern town is populous. It is situated ninety miles west-north-west of Diarbekir.
