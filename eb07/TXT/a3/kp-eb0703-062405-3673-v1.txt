ARNSTADT, a bailiwick in the principality of Schwaz-burg-Sanderhausen, in Germany. It contains 2 cities, 26 villages, and 5 hamlets, with 9700 inhabitants. The capital of the bailiwick is of the same name. It is divided by the river Werra into two portions, has walls, and a castle, in which a gallery of pictures is preserved, and where a porcelain manufactory is now carried on.
