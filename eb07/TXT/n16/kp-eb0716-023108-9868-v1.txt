NIONS, an arrondissement of the department of the Dome, in France, 470 square miles in extent. It comprehends four cantons, divided into seventy-five communes, and containing a population of 33,800 persons. The capital is the city of the same name situated on the river Aigues. It contains only 2850 inhabitants, who carry on. some trade in silks, woollens, and leather,.
