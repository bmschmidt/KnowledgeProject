DEAFNESS, the state of a person who wants the sense of hearing; or the disease of the ear, which prevents the due reception and discrimination of sounds. See Medicine.

. DEALS, a thin kind of fir-planks, of great use in carpentry. They are formed by sawing the trunk of a tree irsto a great many longitudinal divisions, of more or less thickness according to the purposes which they are intended to serve.
