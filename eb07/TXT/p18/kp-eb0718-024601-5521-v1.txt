POL DE LEON, a city of France, in the department of Finisterre, and arrondissement of Morlaix. It is situated on a hill near the sea, about a mile from its haven Pen-pont, which is accessible to small vessels. It is the see of a bishop, and has a cathedral and other churches, 1005 houses, and 6150 inhabitants, who make linen goods, and some pottery ware. Long. 3. 53. 26. W. Lat. 48. 40. 55. N.
