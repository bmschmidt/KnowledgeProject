PORETSCIIIE, a city of the Russian empire, in the province of Smolensko, the capital of a circle of the same name. It is situated on the river Kasplea, which is navigable thusfar, but no farther. It is 478 miles from St. Petersburg, and favourably situated asa depot for the trade between Smolensko and Riga. It contains three churches, 580 houses, and 5900 inhabitants, with large storehouses.
