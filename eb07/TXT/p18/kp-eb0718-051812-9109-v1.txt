PREPOSITION, in Grammar, is one of the parts of speech, being an indeclinable particle which yet serves to govern the nouns that follow it; such as per, pro,propter, through, for, with. Buffier allows it to be only a modificative of a part of speech, serving to circumstantiate a noun.
