PATERNOSTERS, Great, a cluster of shoals and islets in the Eastern Seas, which are about 120 miles in length from north-east to south-west, and thirty in breadth. Long. 117. 10. to 119. 10. E. Lat. 6. 30. to 7. 42. S.

Paternosters, Little. These consist of about thirteen small rocks in the Eastern Seas, between the islands of Borneo and Celebes. They are covered with trees, and have navigable channels between them; but they are surrounded with numerous shoals very dangerous to mariners.
