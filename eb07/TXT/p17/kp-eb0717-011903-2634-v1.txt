PATARA, or Patera, a town on the sea-coast of Caramania, in Asiatic Turkey. It had in ancient times a celebrated oracle of Apollo, and still retains traces of its ancient grandeur. It is now uninhabited, only a few solitary peasants being seen wandering on the plain. Loner. 29. 20. E. Lat. 36. 15. N.
