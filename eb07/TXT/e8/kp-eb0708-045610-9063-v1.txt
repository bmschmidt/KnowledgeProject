EGGENFELDEN, a bailiwick of the circle of the Lower Danube, in the kingdom of Bavaria, extending over 220 square miles. It contains five market-towns, 133 villages, and 867 hamlets, with 22,566 inhabitants. The [7:8:457] capital is the town of the same name. It is situated on the river Roth, and contains 226 houses, with 1340 inhabitants.
