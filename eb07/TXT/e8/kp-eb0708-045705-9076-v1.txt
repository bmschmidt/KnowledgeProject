EGREMONT, a market-town of the ward of Allerdale, in Cumberland, two miles from "the sea, and 304 from London. It consists chiefly of one spacious street, many of the houses having a piazza in front, and bearing marks of considerable antiquity. At the west end of the town are the ruins of a castle. The market is held on Saturday. Population in 1821, 1741, and in 1831, 1741.
