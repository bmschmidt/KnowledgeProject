MONTMORILLON, an arrondissement of the department of the Vienne, in France, extending over 797 square miles. It contains six cantons, divided into 65 communes, inhabited by 49,500 persons. The capital is the city of the same name, situated on the river Gartempe, by which it is divided into two parts. It contains 460 houses, and 3250 inhabitants.
