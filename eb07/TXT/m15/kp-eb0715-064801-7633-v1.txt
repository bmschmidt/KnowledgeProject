MUSSOMELI, a city in the island of Sicily, in the kingdom of Naples, and province of Mazzara, fifty miles from Palermo. It stands on the side of a large inland mountain; and the inhabitants, who are 9276, depend chiefly on the cultivation of the surrounding soil.
