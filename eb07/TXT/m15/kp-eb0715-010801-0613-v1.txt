MILVERTON, a town of the county of Somerset, in the hundred of its own name, 152 miles from London, and three from Wellington. It was a borough, but lost its franchise many years ago. It was once a place where woollen cloths, serges, druggets, and other goods, were extensively made, but the trade bas greatly fallen off of late years. There is a market on Friday. The population amounted in 1801 to 1667, in 1811 to 1637, in 1821 to .1930, and in 1831 to 2233.
