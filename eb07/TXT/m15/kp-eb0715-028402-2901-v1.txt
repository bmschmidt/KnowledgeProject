MINTURNAe, a town of Campania, between Sinuessa and Formiae. It was in the marshes in this neighbourhood that Marius concealed himself in the mud, to avoid the partizans of Sylla. The people condemned him to death; but when the sound of his imperious voice had terrified the executioner, they showed themselves compassionate, and favoured his escape.
