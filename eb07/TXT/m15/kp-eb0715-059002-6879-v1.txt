MUGLITZ, a city of the circle of Olmutz, in the Austrian province of Moravia. It stands on the river March, is surrounded with walls, and is the see of an archbishop, whose cathedral is there. It contains 368 houses, and 3013 inhabitants, some of whom are occupied in woollen manufactures.
