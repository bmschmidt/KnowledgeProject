MOREBAT, a sea-port of Arabia, situated on the southern coast, on a bay bounded by a cape of the same name. It is a poor place; and the inhabitants carry on little trade either by sea or land with the interior. The town is about two miles south of the Cape. Long. 55.4. E. Lat. 17. N.
