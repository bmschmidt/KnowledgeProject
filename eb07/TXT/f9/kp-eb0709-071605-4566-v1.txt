FONG-HOA-TCHING, a large, populous, and commercial town of Chinese Tartary, on the borders of Corea. It has manufactures of white and transparent paper, fabricated from cotton, which is used in windows instead of glass. Long. 143. 42. E. Lat. 40. 31. N.
