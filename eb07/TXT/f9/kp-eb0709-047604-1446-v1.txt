FAILSWORTH, a township of the county of Lancaster, within the parish of Manchester, inhabited chiefly by manufacturers of cotton goods, whose numbers amounted in 1801 to 2622, in 1811 to 2875, in 1821 to 3358, and in 1831 to 3667.
