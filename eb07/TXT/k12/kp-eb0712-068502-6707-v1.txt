KEGWORTH, a parish in the county of Leicester, in the hundred of West Goseote, 114 miles from London. It stands on the river Trent, over whieh is Cavendish Bridge, built by 7 the Duke of Devonshire, to whom a toll is paid. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 1360, in 1811 to 1550, in 1821 to 1607, and in 1831 to 1840.
