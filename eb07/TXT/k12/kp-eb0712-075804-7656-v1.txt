KOPAUL, a town of Hindustan, belonging to the nizam, in the province of Bejapoor, and district of Gujundergur. It is one of the strongest places in the south of India. The lower fort is a semicircle at the bottom of a steep, rocky mountain, commanded by a middle and upper fort, the last overlooking the whole. It is chiefly formed of one immense rock, almost perpendicular, and of great height.
