KAUFBEUERN, a city of Bavaria, in the province of the Upper Danube, and situated on the river Bertoch. It is surrounded with walls, and contains one Lutheran and two Catholic churches, with 490 houses, and 4630 inhabitants. It has a brisk trade in cotton goods, linens, ironmongery, and paper, and Morocco leather and printed calicoes are also made. Long. 10.31. 25. E. Lat. 47. 53.10. N.
