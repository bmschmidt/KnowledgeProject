BALACHNA, a circle of the Russian government of Nishegorod, containing about 90,000 inhabitants. The chief place is a city of the same name, at the junction of the river Usola with the Wolga. It contains about 4500 inhabitants, who carry on a trade in linen, and in salt produced fi'om springs near it. Long. 43. 25. E. Lat. 56. 15. N.
