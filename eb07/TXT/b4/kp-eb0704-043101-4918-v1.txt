BASMAN, a village of Persia, in the province of Khorassan and district of Kohistan, consisting of about 150 houses, and governed by a ehief of the tribe of Beloches. Here is a singular hot well, about twelve yards in circumference, in the eentre of whieh the water boils up with violence from a pipe of burnt elay. There is a tradition that the eity of the Guebres once stood here. Long. 59. 52. E. Lat. 28. 36. N. -
