BACKSCHISARAI, a city in the European Russian government of Taurida, formerly the capital of the peninsula, and the residence of the khan. It was formerly a place of considerable trade, and in 1793 contained 1560 houses, with 5777 inhabitants. It has. however, greatly echned since the peninsula was transferred to Russia. Long. 38. 39.
