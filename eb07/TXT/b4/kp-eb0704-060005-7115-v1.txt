BESHESK, a circle in the Russian government ofTwer, containing 16,457 square miles, with two cities and 171 parishes, and a population of 169,500 persons. The chief place, of the same name, is on the river Mologa, and contains about 4000 inhabitants, employed chiefly in the linen manufacture. Long. 36. 45. E. Lat. 57. 55. N.
