DOBSCHAU, a town of Hungary, in the provinee of Hither Theis. It is situated on the banks of the Dobsina. It is the chief place of the mining district Rosneau, in which are extracted iron, eopper, eobalt, and mereury. In the neighbourhood asbestos and granite are found. It eontains one Catholic and one Lutheran church, with about 500 houses and 4000 inhabitants, mostly Germans, who find employment in manufacturing the metals.
