DOWAGER, Dotisca (a widow endowed, or that has a jointure), a title, or addition, applied to the widows of princes, dukes, earls, and persons of high rank only.

Queen dowager is the widow of the king, and as such she enjoys most of the privileges belonging to her as queen consort.
