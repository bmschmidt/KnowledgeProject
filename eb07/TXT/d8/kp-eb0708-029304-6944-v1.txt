DUTY, in general, denotes any thing that one is obliged to perform.
[7:8:294]

Duty, in commerce, signifies an impost laid on merchandise, at importation or exportation, commonly called the duties of customs; also the taxes of excise, stamp duties, &c. See Political Economy, and Taxation.
