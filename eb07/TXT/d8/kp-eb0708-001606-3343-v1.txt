DIEKIRCH, a city, the capital of the department of the same name, in the duchy of Luxemburg. The department is divided into six cantons, and contains a population of 47,074 persons. The city is situated on the river Eure, and contains 2627 inhabitants, who live by preparing gypsum, and by tanning leather.
