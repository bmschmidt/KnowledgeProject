DOFAR, or Dafar, a seaport of Arabia, in the province of Hadratnaut, on the west coast of a bay of the same name in the Arabian Sea. The chief export of this town consists of olibanum. It is governed by a scheik, who is a sovereign prince. The inhabitants behave with civility to Europeans. It is 120 miles north from Cape Far tach.
