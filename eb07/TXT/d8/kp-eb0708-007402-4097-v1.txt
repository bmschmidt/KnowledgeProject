DOBRUSHKA, or Dobruska, a town in the Austrian kingdom of Bohemia, in the eircle of Kò’nigingratz, the residence of the prinees Colloredo. It has much trade in eorn, flax, and yarn, whieh are largely sold at its weekly market. It contained, in 1819, about 400 houses and 1893 inhabitants.
