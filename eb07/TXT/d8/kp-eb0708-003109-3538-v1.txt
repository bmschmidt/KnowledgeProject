DIMOTIKA, a city in the Turkish province of Gallipoli, where the rivers MoritzÎ± and Kisilnehr join. It contains about 8000 inhabitants, partly Greeks. The chief employment consists in making silk and cotton goods. The citadel served as a residence for the grand signior before the conquest of Constantinople.
