ESCHENBACH, a bailiwick of the circle of the Upper Maine, in the kingdom of Bavaria, extending 264 square miles. It contains two cities, three towns, 166 villages, and 2480 houses, with 15,316 inhabitants. The chief town, of the same name, once fortified, containing 180 houses, and 1056 inhabitants, employed in making linen cloth.
