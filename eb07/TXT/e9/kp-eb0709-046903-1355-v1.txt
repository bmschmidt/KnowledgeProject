EXTRACTION, in Chemistry and Pharmacy, the operation by which essences, tinctures, and the like, are drawn from natural bodies.

Extraction, in genealogy, implies the stock or family from which a person is descended.

Extraction of Roots, in Algebra and Arithmetic, is the method of finding the roots of given numbers or quantities. See Algebra and Arithmetic.
