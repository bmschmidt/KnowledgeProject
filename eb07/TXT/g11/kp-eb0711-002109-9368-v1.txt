GUETARIA, a town of Spain, in lat. 43.18. 30. N., in the province of Guipuscoa, with a port in the Bay of Biscay[7:11:22]. It is remarkable as the birthplace of a celebrated navigator, Don Juan Sebastian del Cano, whose statue is in the principal square. It contains 300 families.
